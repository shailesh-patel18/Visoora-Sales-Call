# Product-Market Fit (PMF) Review

## The Promise
"Your AI Growth Strategist That Understands Your Business First."

## The Reality
Currently, the product feels like a wrapper around an email sender. The "Business Brain" concept is powerful, but the execution is hidden from the user. 

### What's Missing for PMF?
1. **The "Aha!" Moment:** Users need to see *why* Visoora is better than Instantly or Apollo. If the AI doesn't explicitly show its reasoning ("I targeted John because his company just raised Series A and your Business Brain states you excel with funded startups"), it's just another black-box outreach tool.
2. **The "Revenue First" Dashboard:** The dashboard shows arbitrary metrics ("Companies Researched"). CEOs don't care about companies researched; they care about Pipeline Velocity and Meetings Booked. The dashboard must immediately show ROI.
3. **Control (Approval Cockpit):** Sales leaders are terrified of AI sending hallucinatory emails to key prospects. The current system does not provide a robust enough Diff/Inline edit interface to give them confidence.
4. **Mission Replay:** When a mission fails or succeeds, the user must be able to "Replay" the AI's thought process. This builds the ultimate trust.

## PMF Hardening Actions
- Build the **Approval Cockpit** immediately. It is the core feature that mitigates customer fear.
- Expose the **Business Brain** logic on every generated draft so the user sees the AI actually *using* their context.
