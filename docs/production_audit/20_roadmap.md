# 12-Month Roadmap

## Next 30 Days: "The Hardened Core"
**Goal:** Prepare for 20 Pilot Customers safely.
- **Security:** RLS enforced, Edge Middleware fixed, Localhost backdoors removed.
- **Trust:** Resend integrated, custom branded emails live.
- **Scalability:** Celery/BullMQ implemented for async AI missions.
- **UX:** Approval Cockpit V1 built.

## Next 90 Days: "Explainability & PMF"
**Goal:** Prove ROI and secure high retention.
- **Mission Replay:** Full audit logs of AI reasoning presented to the user.
- **Revenue Dashboard:** Integration with CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce) to pull actual booked pipeline values.
- **Multi-Model Fallbacks:** Anthropic + OpenAI auto-failover to ensure 99.9% uptime for the Mission Engine.

## Next 6 Months: "The Autonomous Agent"
**Goal:** Reduce manual approval fatigue.
- **Confidence Thresholds:** Users can set a rule: "If AI confidence is > 95%, auto-send. If < 95%, require approval."
- **Inbound Processing:** AI reads replies, classifies intent (Meeting, Objection, Unsubscribe), and drafts the response.

## 1 Year: "The Revenue OS"
**Goal:** Visoora becomes the system of record for outbound growth.
- **Omnichannel:** AI executes missions across Email, LinkedIn, and SMS.
- **Continuous Learning:** The Business Brain auto-updates based on which objections are most frequently encountered in the wild.
